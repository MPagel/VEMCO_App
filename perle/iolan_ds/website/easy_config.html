IOLAN EasyConfig Wizard

Launch EasyConfig Wizard

The EasyConfig Wizard allows you to quickly and easily assign an IP address and configure your IOLAN's serial port(s) to one of the following:

  • Console Management
  • TruePort (Virtual COM Port)
  • TCP Sockets (Raw TCP)
  • Terminal
  • Printing
  • Serial Tunneling
